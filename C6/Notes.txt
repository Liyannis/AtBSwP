escape chars:
	\'	single quote
	\"	double quote
	\t	tab
	\n	newline (line break)
	\\	backslash
	
raw strings:
	print(r'example\'s')
	
multiline strings with triple quotes:
	print('''something 
			something
			example''')
	
Multiline Comments:
	"""comments
	that explain"""

Indexing and Slicing Strings:
	strings are like lists in regards of indexing.
	
in and not in Operators with Strings:
	case sensitive
	evaluate to a boolean True or False

	join(), split()
	rjust(), ljust(), center()
